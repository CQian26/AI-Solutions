STATEMENT OF WORK — BRACKET, VEHICULAR (NSN 2590-01-701-2434)

1. Scope.  The Contractor shall manufacture and deliver Bracket, Vehicular
   assemblies in accordance with drawing package attached to this solicitation.

2. Specialty Metals.  The Contractor shall comply with DFARS 252.225-7008
   Restriction on Acquisition of Specialty Metals and, where applicable,
   DFARS 252.225-7009 Restriction on Acquisition of Certain Articles
   Containing Specialty Metals.  A domestic melt certificate is required
   with each shipment.

3. Hexavalent Chromium.  The Contractor shall not use Hexavalent Chromium
   in any deliverable in accordance with DFARS 252.223-7008 Prohibition of
   Hexavalent Chromium.  Written approval of the Contracting Officer is
   required for any exception.

4. Sources of Electronic Parts.  DFARS 252.246-7008 Sources of Electronic
   Parts applies to any electronic items procured under this contract.

5. Rights in Technical Data.  DFARS 252.227-7013 Rights in Technical
   Data—Noncommercial Items applies to any technical data delivered.

6. Notification of Potential Safety Issues.  DFARS 252.246-7003 applies —
   this is a critical safety item.

7. Wide Area Workflow.  Payment requests and receiving reports shall be
   submitted electronically per DFARS 252.232-7003 and DFARS 252.232-7006.
